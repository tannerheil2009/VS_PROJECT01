Civillian vs Millitary purposes

Both clearly having two different purposes:

Civillian tires are built for fuel-efficiency, traction on road pavements, comfort, etc. Meanwhile, Milliraty tires are meant for off-road driving, increasiong survivability; being more thicker overall, being able to drive after a flat tire, and others.
